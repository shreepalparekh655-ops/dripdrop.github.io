Welcome to Drip Drop

Drip Drop is a cutting-edge tech-enabled laundry service designed for the modern busy student and professional. Our goal is to take the hassle out of laundry by offering contactless pick-up and delivery, real-time tracking, personalized laundry care recommendations, and hygienic handling, all accessible via a friendly mobile app. Whether you need a one-time wash or a subscription plan, Drip Drop fits your schedule and budget.

About Us

Founded with the vision of revolutionizing laundry services, Drip Drop connects you to trusted laundry vendors via our easy-to-use app. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing, exceptional customer support, and sustainable hygiene practices such as UV sterilization and sealed packaging. Our platform leverages AI to offer personalized wash cycles while minimizing water and energy consumption.

Drip Drop not only helps save time and effort, it offers peace of mind with contactless transactions and secure digital payments, making laundry day seamless and stress-free. Join thousands of satisfied customers who trust Drip Drop to keep their clothes fresh.
