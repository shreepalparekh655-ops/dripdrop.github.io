Welcome to Drip Drop

Drip Drop is a cutting-edge tech-enabled laundry service designed for the modern busy student and professional.

Our mission is to make laundry effortless, with contactless pickups, hygienic packaging, and reliable delivery scheduled around your convenience.

Integrated real-time tracking and AI driven wash care ensure a seamless and personalized service experience every time.

About Us

Founded to revolutionize traditional laundry services, Drip Drop partners with trusted vendors to offer you app-based booking, transparent pricing, and sustainable, contactless operations.

With UV sterilization, sealed packaging, and AI personalized recommendations, we minimize your workload and environmental impact for healthier clothes and planet.

Our commitment extends beyond laundry to customer support and secure, digital payments, giving you peace of mind from scheduling to doorstep delivery.
