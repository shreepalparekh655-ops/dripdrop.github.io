Welcome to Drip Drop

Drip Drop is a cutting-edge tech-enabled laundry service designed for busy students and professionals who value convenience and quality.

Our mission is to simplify your laundry experience with seamless app-based booking, contactless pickups, hygienic handling, and reliable delivery scheduled around your day.

Enjoy personalized wash care powered by AI and real-time tracking so your clothes get the treatment they deserve.

About Us

Founded to transform the laundry industry, Drip Drop connects you with trusted vendors through an intuitive app that provides transparent pricing, eco-friendly processes, and contactless interactions.

From UV sterilization of garments to sealed packaging and AI-recommended wash settings, we prioritize both your health and your clothing.

Our customer-first approach ensures secure, digital payments and around-the-clock support so every laundry day is stress free.
